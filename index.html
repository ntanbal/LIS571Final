ETHICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

In today's digital world, ethical considerations are crucial in every aspect of technology. Our goal is to explore the ethical issues in various fields of IT, including personal ethics, ethical hacking, data protection, artificial intelligence, and digital literacy.


We will we uncover the ethical dilemmas and challenges facing individuals and organizations in the digital age. Ranging from protecting personal data to ensuring fairness in AI algorithms, we'll discuss how ethical principles shape the way we use and develop technology.
