WHAT IS ETHICS

Technology is rapidly advancing, with new models and systems coming out faster and faster, being used by more and more people. As the prevalence of technology continues to rise, ideas about the right and wrong ways to use it develop too. The technology industry must be held accountable for its practices, products, and their impacts on society. But how and to what extent this must be done is still being discussed, especially as the ever changing nature of technology makes it difficult for standards to address every concern.

Type Description
Utilitarianism Focuses on maximizing overall happiness or utility.
Deontology Emphasizes following moral rules or duties.
