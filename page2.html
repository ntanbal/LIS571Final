ETHICAL HACKING

Hacking is the act of illegally breaking into a computer system (Evans, Martin & Poatsy, 2021, p. 329). While many people may be familiar with the harm hacking causes, such as stealing information or damaging property, there are other types of hackers who consider themselves to be ethical hackers. Ethical hackers, also referred to as white-hat hackers, break into computer systems for benevolent reasons including to help find security vulnerabilities within the system and expose them to the companies that own the systems and their users (Evans, Martin & Poatsy, 2021, p. 329). An important distinction to make is that typically white-hat hackers work with the organizations they are hacking into, making their actions legal
