ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

The ethics of using artificial intelligence (AI) in daily computing, particularly in content moderation on social media platforms, raise important questions. AI algorithms play a significant role in managing various aspects of social media, including content creation, monitoring, and ad management. However, the use of AI in content moderation prompts ethical considerations.

The ethics of AI-driven content moderation on social media platforms are debated. Key questions include whether content moderation is ethical and if AI should be used for it. While moderation involves actions like banning accounts or removing content, AI's ability to understand nuanced language and cultural context is limited.
