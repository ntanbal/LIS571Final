DIGITAL LITERACY

Daily life in a digital world requires literacy and awareness. Especially with the introduction of AI, here are some considerations when engaging with others on the internet:

  1. Learn to identify AI-generated content:
    Ensure that you do not misrepresent a situation by sharing this content maliciously. Deep fakes have become more realistic as AI advances, but you can still inspect these portrayals by zooming in on eyes, teeth, fingers; or by searching the internet for reliable sources

  2. Generate personal AI content according to ethics:
    Exercise caution when generating your own AI content. If a website offers AI generation for free, you are the commodity it is pursuing.

  3. Build your algorithmic awareness:
    Better understanding of how AI works ultimately empowers consumers each opportunity they have to interact with it.
